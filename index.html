Should the Death Penalty be Reinstated

The death penalty was first abolished from Australian culture in 1973 officially however, the last ever legal death penalty fulfilled was in 1967. The idea of having the death penalty was to limit the amount of crime that was around the area. The topic has arisen now in 2017-2018 asking the question, should the death penalty be reinstated in Australia. A person would only be sentenced to the penalty if they committed a serious crime which would be decided by the government.
